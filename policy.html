Policy

Below is the policy in place for Edith's Dance Studio.

Behavior

During class time we ask that the following behavior be used by all attendees:

  • Turn cell phone volume down low
  • Refrain from eating or drinking in the studio. Water is permitted.
  • Please arrive on time.

Attire

Wear comfortable clothing that will allow you to stretch and move. The following rules need to be observed:

  1. Shirts or tops at all times.
  2. No sandals
  3. Non-dangly jewelry is preferred. Keep expensive jewelry at home.

There is a lost-and-found bin in case you have misplaced an item.

We have a list of stores that provide dance attire that is suitable for class if you are interested.

Class Make Up

You can make up 1 dance class per eight week dance session. No exceptions.

Refunds

A full refund is permitted up to one week after the start of class.

We look forward to working with you and make every effort to have a good time in class. Please contact us if you have any comments or suggestions.
